The ETL algorithm for updateable tables, algorithm #6, was updated to treat an
edge case for when rows are UPDATEd after they are INSERTed, but before the
UPDATE phase of the algorithm starts.


Three columns have been added to the etl.table_state table of the etl_mgmt 
database in order to store CDC state between runs of the ETL job:

	target_synced_to_insert_id                     | bigint                      | 
	target_synced_to_last_updated_on               | timestamp without time zone | 
	target_unsynced_inserts_from_last_updated_on   | timestamp without time zone |
