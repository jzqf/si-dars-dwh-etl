No new tables or columns were added to any of the operational databases and no
changes were made to the data models of the dwh_psa_obo and dwh_dsa_obo
databases.

This release added support for "run states" for ETL jobs. This required the
following changes:

1.	Modified etl_mgmt data model:

	a.	New columns for table etl.etl_run
	
			run_state_id
			run_state_set_on
	
	b.	Renamed etl.etl_run column:
	
			shutdown_requested_reason -> shutdown_reason
	
	c.	New table:
	
			etl.run_state
	
2.	Updated logic in PDI jobs & transformations.
